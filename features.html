Features built around real daily work.

Daily Ops Report Bot combines inventory, orders, employees, customers, invoices, barcodes, purchase orders, reporting, audits, and backups into one Windows desktop application.

1

Inventory with photos

Add product photos so employees can identify stock quickly. Track quantity, default quantity, notes, status, due date, categories, filters, and more.

2

Categories, filters, and locations

Owners and managers can create the categories and filters that match their business. Track whether stock is on the sales floor, back room, shelf, warehouse, or service vehicle.

3

Item variants

Track color, size, and SKU so businesses can manage stock like black medium shirts separately from red large shirts.

4

Orders and invoices

Create customer orders with due dates, customer email, item lines, prices, totals, and a professional invoice template with business info.

5

Customer history

Review customers, order count, open orders, completed orders, total items ordered, and last order date.

6

Today’s Work

Give employees a simplified screen to see available work, assign an order to themselves, view items, and mark work complete.

7

Barcodes

Print inventory barcodes, scan items into orders, and use advanced barcode actions for faster stock movement.

8

Reorders and purchase orders

Generate reorder requests from low-stock items and create purchase orders grouped by vendor.

9

Audit trail and backups

Track important activity and protect business data with backup and restore tools.

Screenshot placeholders

Replace these files in assets/images/screenshots with real program screenshots whenever you are ready.
