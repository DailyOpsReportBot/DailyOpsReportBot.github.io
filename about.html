Created to help small businesses work with more confidence.

Daily Ops Report Bot was created by Tim McIntyre in 2026 as a practical desktop application for inventory-based small businesses.

What Daily Ops Report Bot does

Daily Ops Report Bot helps small businesses manage inventory, customer orders, employee work, invoices, barcodes, purchase orders, audit history, reports, and backups in one organized Windows desktop application.

It is designed for non-restaurant businesses that keep regular stock on hand, such as clothing boutiques, repair shops, flooring companies, home improvement businesses, supply stores, parts sellers, print shops, beauty supply stores, and small warehouses.

When it was created

Daily Ops Report Bot was created in 2026 as a practical business application built around the needs of small businesses that want better inventory and order control without a complicated enterprise system.

Why it was created

The application was created to help small businesses stop relying only on spreadsheets, paper notes, whiteboards, and memory. The goal was to build a clear, useful system that helps owners and managers see what is in stock, what needs attention, who is working on each order, and what has been completed.

About the creator

Daily Ops Report Bot was created by Tim McIntyre from LaGrange, Georgia. Tim served four years in the United States Marine Corps. After leaving the Marines, he pursued a career in Software Engineering and Development.

He wanted to use his knowledge to build something practical that could help small businesses in his hometown of LaGrange, GA become more organized, more accountable, and better prepared for daily operations.
